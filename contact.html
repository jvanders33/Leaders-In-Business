Leaders in Business helps accounting firms turn strategic advisory into a structured, repeatable and commercially viable capability. Follow the certification pathway to build skills, or licence the Strategic Advisory Model to implement that structure across your firm.

Support doesn't stop after training. You'll build momentum through the Advisor Club, ongoing resources and the Advisory Workspace, plus access to dedicated advisors who can guide implementation, troubleshoot real client situations and help strengthen your delivery over time.

There are two clear entry points: learn the method through Advisory Certification Training (Level 1 or 2), or licence the Strategic Advisory Operating Model. If you're unsure which fits your firm, book a call and we'll help you choose the right next step.
