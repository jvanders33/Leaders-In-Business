frequently asked questions

Leaders in Business helps accounting firms turn strategic advisory into a structured, repeatable and commercially viable capability — not something dependent on one partner or delivered inconsistently.

You can follow the certification pathway (Level 1, 2 and 3) to build advisory confidence and delivery skills, or licence the Strategic Advisory Model to implement that structure across your firm. Training builds capability; licensing enables implementation.

It's both, depending on where you start. Advisory Certification Training builds your personal capability and confidence. Licensing the Strategic Advisory Model lets your firm implement that structure at scale using the Advisory Workspace and ongoing support. You can complete training without licensing the model.

Practical training designed to help accountants deliver stronger strategic conversations with clients. You'll learn the structure, practise the method and walk away with certification recognition and tools you can apply immediately. Three certification levels are available depending on where you're at.

Level 1 (Advisory Method Certification) is a short, practical online session giving you the foundations — the structure, language and flow behind strong advisory conversations. Level 2 (Advisory Delivery Certification) is a deeper, immersive workshop for accountants ready to practise real delivery, work through client scenarios and package advisory as a repeatable service.

Structured diagnostics, session templates, planning tools, dashboards, action tracking, client-ready resources, AI agent tools and more — all designed to support advisory conversations before, during and after meetings, turning insight into documented actions and measurable progress.

It's designed for both newcomers and those already delivering advisory. If you're starting out, you'll learn a clear method to run structured conversations with confidence. If you're already advising, it helps you formalise and strengthen what you do — turning informal sessions into a repeatable service clients clearly value.
